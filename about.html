About Us

Hello! Welcome to Wallys Adventure! This is a simple arcade game that features Wally the Steam Whistle, the last steam whistle to be heard in Trenton. In this game, Wally's exploring different parts of modern Trenton and in the process, he has to dodge the larger buildings and planes that now come by the place. The idea for this game came from a class project that explored the relationship between Wally and the man who preserved him, William Lloyd Miller. William Miller was responsible for closing down the factory where Wally was located. Similar to how William never forgot about the history of Wally, this game serves to preserve the history of both William and Wally. Enjoy the game and view the links below!
