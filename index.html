Wally's Adventure

Instructions:

Goal: Try to get as far you can with Wally the Steamwhistle.
Press enter to start and restart the game!
Use the spacebar/ left click to jump, and the down arrow key to duck.
Get to level 2 to hear Wally and get to level 3 to see the planes!
